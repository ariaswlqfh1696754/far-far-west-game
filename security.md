### How to Tame a Wild Horse

1. Find wild horse herd (plains biome)
2. Sneak (hold Ctrl) to approach slowly
3. Equip **Lasso** (press 4)
4. Hold right-click to swing, release to throw
5. Horse is roped — hold RMB to keep tension
6. Walk toward horse while holding RMB
7. When close, press E to mount
8. Hold on (move mouse opposite horse's buck)
9. Success → horse is now yours!

### How to Start Ranching

1. Build or buy a **Barn** ($500)
2. Buy cattle from **Livestock Trader**
3. Build **Fence** enclosure (prevents wandering)
4. Feed animals: hay, grain, or let them graze
5. Animals grow over 20-30 in-game days
6. Sell grown animals for profit
7. Protect from predators (wolves, bears)

### How to Participate in Cattle Drive

1. Own at least 10 adult cattle
2. Talk to **Trail Boss** in town
3. Accept mission (destination is 2-5 km away)
4. Herd cattle using horse and calls
5. Camp overnight (defend from rustlers)
6. Complete drive in 3-5 in-game days
7. Get paid ($200-800 depending on cattle condition)

### How to Duel

1. Accept duel challenge (or provoke antagonist)
2. Face opponent (both hands on holster)
3. Hold right-click (hand hovers over gun)
4. Screen shows tension meter
5. When opponent reaches, draw (left-click)
6. Aim at opponent's chest/head
7. Shoot before opponent fires
8. Quickest draw wins!

### How to Build Your Town

1. Find open land (no existing buildings within 200m)
2. Craft **Town Claim Flag** (cost: 10 wood, 5 stone)
3. Place flag to claim territory
4. Build **Town Hall** (foundation building)
5. Attract settlers by completing tasks
6. Construct buildings: homes, stores, saloon
7. Upgrade buildings for better NPC services
8. Defend from bandit raids
